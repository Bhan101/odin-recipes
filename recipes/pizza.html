How to make the Italian pizza

  1. Prepare the dough: In a large bowl, mix the flour and salt together. Add the yeast to warm water and let it stand for a few minutes for bubbles or foam to appear.

    2. Pour yeasty water into the flour bowl slowly, mixing with a spoon at the same time. Once all the water has been added, add olive oil and mix with a spoon to combine

  2. Knead dough: Transfer the dough to a bench thatâ€™s been dusted with flour. Knead with your hands for about 10 minutes until the dough is elastic and smooth.
  3. Resting time: In a large bowl, mix the flour and salt together. Add the yeast to warm water and let it stand for a few minutes for bubbles or foam to appear.

    4. The dough would have doubled in size. Divide into 3 even balls then add a touch of olive oil to the top of the balls and let them rest covered with cling wrap or a damp cloth for a minimum of 2 hours

  4. Make the pizza: In a large bowl, mix the flour and salt together. Add the yeast to warm water and let it stand for a few minutes for bubbles or foam to appear.

    5. To make the pizza shape, dust your bench with semolina flour and using your hands gently stretch into a pizza shape

  5. Add toppings: Add your tomato sauce and favourite toppings, all except the mozzarella cheese. Drizzle with olive oil and brush some onto the crust too.
  6. Cook the pizza: Gently move the pizza base to your heated pizza pan or oven tray. Cook in the oven on the lowest level for approximately 4-5 minutes.

    7. Remove from oven, add mozzarella cheese and place back in the oven for 3-4 minutes until the cheese has melted.

    Tear over some fresh basil leaves and enjoy!
